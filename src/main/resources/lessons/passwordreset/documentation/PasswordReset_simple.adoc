== Email functionality

Let's first do a simple assignment to make sure you are able to read e-mails. The e-mail you receive
during the lessons is delivered to your mailbox in WebGoat, which you open with the envelope (mailbox)
button in the top right corner, next to the language selector.
In the reset page below send an e-mail to `username@webgoat.org` (part behind the @ is not important)
Open your mailbox, read the e-mail and login with your username and the password provided in the e-mail.
